# Governance

## Purpose

The objective of Stakers Union governance is to guide the project towards it primary goals of promoting the interests of solo-stakers (current and future) with a focus on Stakers Union members, and supporting the decentralization of the Ethereum network. The governance structure is designed to ensure that the project is managed in a transparent and accountable manner.

## Structure

The Stakers Union governance structure is organized into [subcommittees](/governance/subcommittees) with associated [leaders](/governance/leadership), each of which is responsible for a specific aspect of the project.
