# Delegation

Delegation for the Stakers Union Snapshot Space is managed <SnapshotLink href={'/delegates'}>here</SnapshotLink>. Delegation interactions are published on Ethereum Mainnet, make sure your POAP-containing wallet is connected to the correct network and has sufficient ETH for gas.

## Delegating Your Voting Power

There are several options for delegating your voting power depending on whether you know the address of the delegatee or if you would like to delegate to an existing delegatee.

### Enter the Delegatee's Address

<div className={'my-4 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg overflow-hidden bg-neutral-800'}>
  <Image
    src={'/delegate-new.png'}
    alt={'Email'}
    width={800}
    height={400}
  />
  <div
    className={
      'text-sm text-neutral-600 dark:text-neutral-300 p-2 bg-neutral-50 dark:bg-neutral-700'
    }
  >
    Select the <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faUserPlus} /> icon in the top right corner of the Snapshot
    page, enter the delegatee's address, and select "Delegate".
  </div>
</div>

### Select an Existing Delegatee

<div className={'my-4 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg overflow-hidden bg-neutral-800'}>
  <Image
    src={'/delegate-existing.png'}
    alt={'Email'}
    width={800}
    height={400}
  />
  <div
    className={
      'text-sm text-neutral-600 dark:text-neutral-300 p-2 bg-neutral-50 dark:bg-neutral-700'
    }
  >
    Select the <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faEllipsis} /> icon to the right of the delegatee's address
    and select "Delegate" in the dropdown menu.
  </div>
</div>

## Undelegating Your Voting Power

<div className={'my-4 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg overflow-hidden bg-neutral-800'}>

    address and select <FontAwesomeIcon icon={faUserMinus} /> "Undelegate" in the dropdown menu.
  </div>
</div>

## Becoming a Delegatee

If you are interested in becoming a delegatee you can customize your profile to include a statement reflecting your voting objectives to guide members in their delegation decisions.

<div className={'my-4 border border-neutral-700 rounded-lg overflow-hidden bg-neutral-800'}>
  <Image
    src={'/delegatee.png'}
    alt={'Email'}
    width={800}
    height={400}
  />
  <div
    className={
      'text-sm text-neutral-600 dark:text-neutral-300 p-2 bg-neutral-50 dark:bg-neutral-700'
    }
  >
    Select the "Edit my statement" button to edit your profile to include a statement reflecting
    your voting objectives.
  </div>
</div>
